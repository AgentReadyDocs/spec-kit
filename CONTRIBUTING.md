# Contributing

Thanks for your interest in contributing.

## Ground Rules

- Do not contribute proprietary, confidential, or customer-identifying material.
- Do not include personal data (PII), credentials, or secrets.
- Keep examples synthetic or fully anonymized.
- Keep contributions aligned with this repository's scope.

## IP And Public Scope

- Contributions are for public, open-source collaboration.
- Do not submit confidential, customer-specific, or non-public business information.
- By contributing, you confirm you have rights to submit the content.

## Workflow

1. Open an issue describing the change.
2. Create a focused branch and commit with clear messages.
3. Open a PR with what changed, why, and how to validate.
4. Optional: use `git commit -s` to sign off commits (DCO-style sign-off).
